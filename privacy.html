Privacy Policy – Ludimus

Ludimus is a mobile application designed for sports clubs to manage training attendance, match schedules, and announcements. The application is intended for use by club members including players, coaches, and parents.

What information do we collect?

  • User account details (name, email, username)
  • Personal data entered by users (birth date, height, weight, etc.)

How do we use your data?

  • To display user profile information
  • To track attendance to trainings and matches
  • To communicate club-related information via announcements

Do we share your data?

No. Your data is only accessible to authorized users within your club.

Data storage

All data is securely stored on our servers and only accessible via login.

Contact

For questions or concerns, contact us at: support@ludimus.app

Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Please check this page regularly.
